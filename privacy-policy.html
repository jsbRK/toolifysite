Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to ToolifySite ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe experience on our website, https://toolifysite.com/. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data (or lack thereof).

2. No Personal Data Collection

One of our core principles is "No Signup, No Login." We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address. You can use all our tools anonymously.

3. How Our Tools Work (Client-Side Processing)

Your security is our top priority. For the vast majority of our tools (including Image Tools, PDF Converters, and Text formatters), the processing happens entirely within your browser (Client-Side).

  • Your files (images, PDFs, documents) are NOT uploaded to our servers.
  • Processing is done locally on your device.
  • Once you close the tab, the data is gone.

4. Third-Party Services & Cookies

While we don't collect data, we may use third-party services to keep the site running for free:

  • Analytics: We may use anonymous analytics tools (like Google Analytics) to understand which tools are popular. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.
  • Advertising: We may display ads (via Google AdSense or others) to support server costs. These third-party vendors may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Contact page (coming soon).
